Why PAN Photos Get Rejected

PAN card applications often get delayed because of incorrect photo uploads. Here are the most common reasons:

1. Wrong Dimensions

Using images not matching recommended dimensions (1020 × 648 px).

2. File Size Too Large

Some portals do not accept images above ~200 KB.

3. Shadows or Dark Backgrounds

PAN photos must have a clean, white/light background.

4. Poor Quality Image

Blurry or low-light photos get rejected quickly.

5. Face Not Centered

Improper framing is a common rejection reason.
