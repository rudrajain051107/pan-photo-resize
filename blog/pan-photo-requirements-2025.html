PAN Photo Requirements (2025 Updated)

These are the recommended specifications for uploading a PAN card photo in 2025.

1. Dimensions

For digital uploads, use:

  • 1020 × 648 pixels (recommended)

2. File Format

  • JPG or JPEG

3. File Size

  • Preferably under 200 KB

4. Background

  • White or light-colored
  • No shadows

5. Face Requirements

  • Centered and clear
  • Neutral expression
  • No accessories covering the face
